Welcome to My Simple Webpage

A computer is an electronic device designed to process, store, and manage data. It operates by executing a series of instructions or programs to perform various tasks. Computers come in many forms, from large mainframes to compact laptops and powerful smartphones. They consist of key components such as the central processing unit (CPU), memory (RAM), storage devices (like hard drives or SSDs), and input/output interfaces. Modern computers can handle complex computations, connect to the internet, and run diverse applications, making them essential tools in business, education, entertainment, and many other fields.
